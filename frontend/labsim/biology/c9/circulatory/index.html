🫀 Blood Circulation Simulator

Pulse: 72 bpm

🔴 Oxygenated Blood

Rich in oxygen

Carries oxygen from the lungs to all body cells

Color: Bright red

Route: Lungs → Heart → Body

🔵 Deoxygenated Blood

Low in oxygen

Returns from body cells to the lungs to get oxygenated

Color: Dark blue/purple

Route: Body → Heart → Lungs

❤️ The Heart

4-chambered pump

Left side: Pumps oxygenated blood to body

Right side: Pumps deoxygenated blood to lungs

Role: Maintains continuous circulation
