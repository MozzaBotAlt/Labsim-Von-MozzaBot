About Labsim

LabSim was created by three developers from MRSM Langkawi. It is created to help users learn science and replicate experiment seamlessly.This simulator is created through scratch platform to allow students replicate lab experiments accurately through a 2D graphics. Labsim is using IGCSE Biology textbook as reference. Biology teachers can also use this platform in class as study aids to help students comprehend the topic more or if the teacher has no materials or apparatuses available.

Objectives

  1. Develop an interactive, user-friendly, and scientifically accurate simulator.
  2. Allow students to conduct food tests (starch, protein, reducing sugar, fat) in a digital environment
  3. Replicate the real lab experience virtually while adding educational and competition value.
